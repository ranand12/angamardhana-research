# Angamardana (Full Body Conditioning)

---

## A. Standing Postures

### 1. Calf Raises (V-Stance)

**Stance:** Stand with heels touching, toes pointing outward in a V-shape.

**Breath:** Inhale as you go up, stay for a few deep breaths, exhale as you come down.

**Posture 1:**
- Start: Arms straight up alongside ears.
- Movement: Lift heels off the ground, balance on toes.
- Return: Lower heels.

**Posture 2:**
- Start: Interlace fingers, place hands firmly behind neck/ears.
- Movement: Lift heels, balance on toes.
- Return: Lower heels.

**Posture 3:**
- Start: Reach hands behind back, grab forearms (criss-cross).
- Movement: Lift heels, balance on toes.
- Return: Lower heels.

**Posture 4:**
- Start: Interlace fingers, press palms up toward ceiling.
- Movement: Lift heels, balance on toes.
- Return: Lower heels. Release hands.

---

### 2. Utthita Lolasana Variation (Forward Folds / Swinging While Standing - V-Stance)

**Stance:** Stand with heels touching, toes pointing outward in a V-shape.

**Breath:** Exhale as you go down, then do 3 forced exhales from the pit of your throat with a mild constriction, then inhale as you bring your torso up.

**Posture 1:**
- Start: Arms straight up alongside ears.
- Movement: Fold torso forward and down.
- Return: Stand back up straight.

**Posture 2:**
- Start: Interlace fingers behind neck/ears.
- Movement: Fold torso forward and down.
- Return: Stand back up straight.

**Posture 3:**
- Start: Criss-cross arms behind back.
- Movement: Fold torso forward and down.
- Return: Stand back up straight.

**Posture 4:**
- Start: Interlace fingers, press palms up toward ceiling.
- Movement: Fold torso forward and down.
- Return: Stand back up straight. Release hands.

### 5. Parsvottanasana / Intense Side Stretch Pose (Right Side)

**Stance:** Take a 3-foot step out with right foot. Left foot faces forward; right foot points to the right side. Turn entire torso to face the right side. Keep both legs straight with the front knee fully extended throughout.

**Breath:** Exhale as you go down, then do 3 forced exhales from the pit of your throat with a mild constriction, then inhale as you bring your torso up.

**Posture 1:**
- Start: Arms straight up alongside ears.
- Movement: Fold torso down over right leg.
- Return: Stand back up.

**Posture 2:**
- Start: Interlace fingers behind neck/ears.
- Movement: Fold torso down over right leg.
- Return: Stand back up.

**Posture 3:**
- Start: Criss-cross arms behind back.
- Movement: Fold torso down over right leg.
- Return: Stand back up.

**Posture 4 (Parvatasana arms / overhead interlocked-finger stretch):**
- Start: Interlace fingers, press palms up to ceiling.
- Movement: Fold torso down over right leg.
- Return: Stand back up.

**Posture 5 (Cow Face / Gomukhasana arms):**
- Start: Reach right arm up and drop hand down back. Reach left arm behind and grab right fingers (use a towel if they don't touch).
- Movement: Fold torso down over right leg.
- Return: Stand back up.

**Posture 6 (Reverse Prayer / Paschima Namaskarasana):**
- Start: Place hands behind back, press palms together in prayer position.
- Movement: Fold torso down over right leg.
- Return: Stand back up. Release hands.

### 7. Tiryaka Tadasana (Swaying Palm Tree Pose / Lateral Pulses - Right Side)

**Breath:** Exhale as you go to the side, then do 3 forced exhales from the pit of your throat with a mild constriction, then inhale as you bring your body up.

- Start: Inhale and reach right arm straight up.
- Movement: Exhale and pulse upper body gently toward the left side three times.

## B. Squatting Postures

### 1. Front Leg Extensions

**Stance:** Drop into a low squat, balancing on toes/balls of feet. Keep back straight. Gaze straight ahead. Balance on fingertips, not palms.

**Breath:** Exhale as you extend the leg, hold and breathe normally for 3-5 seconds, inhale and bring leg back to original position.

**Right Leg (3 reps):**
- Start: Low squat on toes, fingertips on floor.
- Movement: Exhale and extend right leg perfectly straight out in front, keeping it off the floor. Hold for 3 seconds.
- Return: Pull leg back into squat.

**Left Leg (3 reps):**
- Start: Low squat on toes, fingertips on floor.
- Movement: Exhale and extend left leg perfectly straight out in front, keeping it off the floor. Hold for 3 seconds.
- Return: Pull leg back into squat.

---

### 2. Side Leg Extensions

**Stance:** Drop into a low squat, balancing on toes/balls of feet. Keep back straight. Gaze straight ahead.

**Breath:** Exhale as you extend the leg, hold and breathe normally for 3-5 seconds, inhale and bring leg back to original position.

**Right Leg (3 reps):**
- Start: Low squat on toes, back straight.
- Movement: Exhale and extend right leg perfectly straight out to the right side, keeping it off the floor.
- Return: Pull leg back into squat.

**Left Leg (3 reps):**
- Start: Low squat on toes, back straight.
- Movement: Exhale and extend left leg perfectly straight out to the left side, keeping it off the floor.
- Return: Pull leg back into squat.

---

### 3. Skandasana Variation (Side-Lunge Hip Lifts)

**Right Side (3 reps):**
- Start: Deep squat on the balls of both feet, right leg extended straight out to the side (right foot flat on floor), left knee in the squat, fingertips on the floor in front.
- Movement: Exhale, straighten the left (squatting) leg to drive the hips up high, folding the torso down toward the extended right leg / nose-to-knee.
- Return: Inhale, drop hips back down to the starting squat position.

**Left Side (3 reps):**
- Start: Deep squat on the balls of both feet, left leg extended straight out to the side (left foot flat on floor), right knee in the squat, fingertips on the floor in front.
- Movement: Exhale, straighten the right (squatting) leg to drive the hips up high, folding the torso down toward the extended left leg / nose-to-knee.
- Return: Inhale, drop hips back down to the starting squat position.

---

### 4. Squat Jumps

**Stance:** Squat with palms on ground and toes on ground. Palms in front of feet. Inhale and pull both legs back into low squat. Arch spine as you bring feet back. Keep palms flat on floor in front.

**Set 1:**
- Start: Take one deep breath in.
- Movement: Without inhaling again, jump feet to the left, jump to the middle, jump to the right.

**Set 2:**
- Start: Deep breath in.
- Movement: Jump left, jump middle, jump right.

**Set 3:**
- Start: Deep breath in.
- Movement: Jump left, jump middle, jump right.

---

### 5. Breathless Push-ups

- Start: From the squat, inhale deeply. Exhale and jump feet back into high plank (push-up position).
- Movement: Perform as many push-ups as you can without taking a single breath.
- End: The exact second you need to breathe, immediately drop knees to the floor and push hips back into Child's Pose.

## C. Sitting Postures

### 1. Dandasana Variation (L-Sit Single-Leg Lifts)

**Stance:** Sit flat on the floor with legs extended straight out in front (90-degree L-shape). Feet flexed (toes pointing up), hands flat on the floor next to hips. Back perfectly straight.

**Right Leg (3 reps):**
- Start: Seated in Dandasana, hands pressing into floor.
- Movement: Inhale and lift straight right leg up off the floor as high as you can without leaning backward.
- Return: Release leg down.

**Left Leg (3 reps):**
- Start: Seated in Dandasana, hands pressing into floor.
- Movement: Inhale and lift straight left leg up off the floor as high as you can.
- Return: Release leg down.

---

### 2. Naukasana (Boat Pose)

**3 reps:**
- Start: Seated on the floor, legs in front.
- Movement: Lift both straight legs into the air (bend knees if too hard), balance on tailbone/sit bones with torso leaning slightly back. Hold this V-shape.
- Return: Lower legs down.

---

### 3. Ubhaya Padangusthasana (Both Big Toes Pose / Toe Pulls)

**Stance:** Seated, grab big toes with the first two fingers of each hand.

**3 reps:**
- Start: Seated, gripping big toes.
- Movement: Inhale, lift both legs into the air. Keep spine perfectly straight. Bend elbows outward to actively pull legs closer to chest.
- Return: Lower legs down.

---

### 6. Virasana / Vajrasana (Seated Rest)

- Start: Bring legs together, roll onto knees. Either sit directly on heels (knees together), or separate feet slightly and lower hips entirely to the floor between heels.
- Position: Rest hands loosely on thighs. Pull shoulders back and down. Keep chin up.
- Focus: Close eyes, focus on the space between eyebrows. Rest here, breathing naturally for a few minutes.
- Exit: Place palms on the floor, lift hips up, and swing legs out from underneath.

## D. Lying Down Postures

**Starting position:** Lie totally flat on back. Keep heels together and arms by sides.

### 1. Supta Pawanmuktasana (Knee to Chest / Leg Lock Pose)

**Right Side (3 reps):**
- Start: Lying flat on back.
- Movement: Bend right knee, use hands to pull it tightly against chest. Lift head off mat and try to touch nose to knee.
- Return: Release head and leg back to floor.

**Left Side (3 reps):**
- Start: Lying flat on back.
- Movement: Bend left knee, pull it to chest, lift nose to knee.
- Return: Release head and leg back to floor.

---

### 2. Jhulana Lurhakanasana (Rocking)

- Start: Sit in squatting position with buttocks just above the floor. Interlock fingers of both hands and clasp around shins just below the knees.
- Movement: Rock the whole body backwards and forwards on the spine. Try to come up into the squatting pose on the feet when rocking forward. 3-5 backward and forward movements.
- Breath: Inhale as you roll backwards. Exhale as you come forward.
- Note: Use a folded blanket to protect the spine. Keep head forward while rocking back. Be careful not to hit the head on the floor.

---

### 3. Jathara Parivartanasana (Revolved Abdomen Pose - Bent-Knee / Chair-Leg Variation)

- Start: Lie flat on back. Lift legs so hips and knees are bent at 90 degrees ("chair legs" / tabletop). Spread arms out wide at shoulder level for support.
- Movement: Exhale and lower both legs together to one side (keeping knees bent and stacked, close to the floor but not resting). Inhale and bring legs back to centre. Repeat to other side.
- Breath: Exhale while lowering legs to the side. Inhale while returning to centre.

---

### 4. Core Sweeps (Leg-Circle / Chakra Padasana-style)

**Rep 1:**
- Start: Inhale and bring both knees to chest.
- Movement: Exhale and push both legs straight out so they hover at a 45-degree angle. Keeping them straight, bring them back to chest. Sweep them out to the right side, close to the ground but off the ground. Bring back closer to ground, then to chest, then 45-degree angle. Repeat to left side.

**Rep 2:**
- Start: Knees to chest.
- Movement: Push out to 45 degrees. Sweep to the right, lift straight up to 90 degrees, lower to the mat.

**Rep 3:**
- Start: Knees to chest.
- Movement: Push out to 45 degrees. Sweep to the right, lift straight up to 90 degrees, lower to the mat.

---

### 5. Supta Udarakarshanasana (Spinal Twists / Sleeping Abdominal Stretch Pose)

*(Also a combination of Supta Udarakarshanasana and Jathara Parivartanasana - supine spinal twist)*

- Start: Bend both knees so feet are flat on mat. Spread arms out wide into a "T" shape, palms facing up.

**Twist 1:**
- Movement: Drop both knees down to the floor on left side. Turn head to look at right hand.

**Twist 2:**
- Movement: Lift knees back to center. Drop both knees to floor on right side. Turn head to look at left hand.

**Twist 3:**
- Movement: Lift knees back to center. Drop to left side again.

**Twist 4:**
- Movement: Lift knees back to center. Drop to right side again.

**Twist 5:**
- Movement: Lift knees back to center. Drop to left side again.

**Twist 6:**
- Movement: Lift knees back to center. Drop to right side again. Return to center.

---

### 6. Yoga Bicycle (Dwichakrikasana-style Cycling)

- Start: Place hands behind head, lift feet off the floor.
- Movement: For exactly 10 seconds, do a rapid, aggressive pedaling motion with legs. As you pedal, twist torso so right elbow firmly strikes left knee, then left elbow strikes right knee. Ensure the non-striking elbow taps the floor on every rep. The leg should not rest on floor.
- Intensity: Go as fast as physically possible for as long as possible.

## E. Balancing & Savasana

### 1. Warrior III Balance

**Right Side:**
- Start: Roll over to right side and push up to standing. Step left foot forward. Inhale and reach both arms straight up by ears.
- Movement: Exhale, engage core, lean torso forward until upper body is parallel to the ground. Simultaneously lift straight right leg up behind until also parallel to the ground. Bring hands together in prayer at forehead. Pull prayer hands down to center of chest. Push prayer hands back out to forehead, then extend them straight out in front.
- Return: Inhale, lift chest, place right foot back down.

**Left Side:**
- Start: Step right foot forward. Inhale arms up.
- Movement: Exhale, lean forward, lift left leg up. Bring hands in prayer to forehead. Pull to chest. Push to forehead. Extend arms straight.
- Return: Step back down.

---

### 2. Savasana (Corpse Pose)

- Start: Lie completely flat on back. Separate legs slightly so they naturally flop open. Rest arms about a foot away from body with palms facing ceiling.
- Active release: Wiggle toes. Rotate ankles in both directions. Squeeze hands into tight fists, then let them fall completely open. Slowly turn head left, right, and back to center.
- Rest: Close eyes and lay perfectly still for 5 minutes. Do not move a single muscle. Let any thoughts that arise pass by without interacting with them.
- Exit: After 5 minutes, take a deep inhale. Slowly bend knees toward chest. Roll entirely onto right side and rest there for a few seconds. Plant hands and slowly push up to a seated position.
